Information

Rome, the Eternal City, is a captivating blend of ancient wonders and modern vibrancy. From the iconic Colosseum and Roman Forum to the Vatican City's artistic treasures, Rome exudes history and culture. Its charming streets, delectable cuisine, and artistic legacy make it an unparalleled destination.

  • 9 Days 8 Nights, Group: 5 - 10 People, Rome

Rome, a living museum, unfolds with the majestic Pantheon and the Spanish Steps. The timeless allure of Trevi Fountain and the Renaissance beauty of Piazza Navona add to its splendor. Amidst its historic treasures, Rome's lively atmosphere, delectable pasta, and gelato make it a city where the past and present harmoniously coexist.

Tour Plan
  • Day 1: Arrival
    Begin your Italian adventure by flying into Rome. A Safari Tourism representative would greet you at the airport and help you with check-in at the hotel. Relax at the hotel after your long and tiring journey with a comfortable overnight stay. Tonight enjoy an Indian Dinner.
  • Day 2: Adventure Beggins
    After a continental breakfast, embark on a guided city tour of Rome and witness sights such as the Colosseum, Arc of Constantine, Roman Forum and Caesar's Forum. Walk past the Venice Square to experience the stark contrast between the architecture of the traditional Palazzio Venezia and the modern Victor Emmanuel Monument. Later, visit the Trevi Fountain, which is considered the most beautiful fountain of Rome (legend has it that if you make a wish and throw a coin in, your wish will come true and you will return to Rome).
  • Day 3: Rome to Pisa
    After a Continental breakfast, Drive to Florence - the capital of the Tuscany Region. Embark on a walking tour of Florence, seeing sights such as, the Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore, Campanile and Baptistery. Enjoy some time in Piazza Della Signoria to view Palazzo Vecchio, the Loggia Dei Lanzi and the Fountain of Neptune. Later, we visit the historic city of Pisa - famous for its architectural beauty. Upon arrival, visit the Campo Dei Miracoli and enjoy stunning views of the Leaning Tower of Pisa, the Cathedral and the Baptistery of Pisa.The Leaning Tower of Pisa is one of the most remarkable architectural structures from medieval Europe. It has mystified and amazed crowds for hundreds of years, and despite having experienced many a calamity during its lifetime, it is still standing. Proceed for an Indian dinner and later check in to the hotel.
  • Day 4:
    After a scrumptious continental breakfast, get set to visit the famous Ferrari Museum in Maranello. See the best display of their famous cars and racing vehicles. Later drive towards Venice Pier. We set sail to the St. Marks Square, which is considered one of the world's most beautiful squares with its boutiques, traditional cafes, and historic buildings. On your city tour see incredible sites such as the Bridge of Sighs, the Ducale Palace, St. Mark's Basilica and the Clock tower which are part of this romantic city. Tonight Indian Dinner and then check in to the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.
  • Day 5:
    After a hearty continental breakfast at the hotel, drive to the beautiful and glamourous town of Como. Spend some time walking around and exploring the place or spend time for the lake. Later drive to Milan for an orientation sightseeing with the breathtaking Duomo and its magnificent square. Also you would see the Scala Museum and Theatre and Galleria Vittorio Emanuele - which is a double arcade in the centre of Milan. After an Indian Dinner and check in to the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.
  • Day 6:
    After a continental breakfast, sit back and relax as we drive into Switzerland. Enjoy the beauty of the lush green landscape as we drive to the Capital city of Bern. Walk around the city exploring the different things on offer. See the famous clock tower, Parliament Building, Bear Pit and other sights in the city. Later to the beautiful resort town of Meiringen. Enjoy an Indian Dinner and check into the hotel.
  • Day 7:
    After a continental breakfast, sit back and relax as we drive into Switzerland. Enjoy the beauty of the lush green landscape as we drive to the Capital city of Bern. Walk around the city exploring the different things on offer. See the famous clock tower, Parliament Building, Bear Pit and other sights in the city. Later to the beautiful resort town of Meiringen. Enjoy an Indian Dinner and check into the hotel.
  • Day 8:
    After a continental breakfast, begin the adventurous journey to Mt. Titlis. On a hair-raising and unforgettable 35 minutes long ride to the top, switching two different cable cars, the ride is nothing short of awe-inspiring as you'll witness the scenery change from green meadows and scenic lakes to snow-clad mountains. The last leg of the ride is on the famous Rotair, which is the world's first revolving cable car that offers beautiful panoramic views of the surrounding glaciers, mountains, and scenery. Once atop Mt. Titlis, what awaits visitors is breathtaking views of the Alps from either the sun terrace or from the famous ’south face window’ of the mountain. After the great Mt. Titlis experience, proceed to Lucerne, a beautiful city located at the northern tip of Lake Lucerne. Stroll along the banks of Lake Lucerne to experience attractions like the Kapellbrucke – The Chapel Bridge, the traditional wooden bridge of Lucerne. Spend some free time shopping at the famous ’Bucherer’ for Swiss watches, jewellery and souvenirs. After an Indian Dinner, check in to the hotel for an overnight stay in Zurich.
  • Day 9: Departure
    After a continental Breakfast, check out and proceed to the airport for your flight back home. Return home with wonderful memories of your tour with Safari Tourism.
