Information

Kashmir, a crown jewel in northern India, enchants with its snow-capped peaks, serene lakes, and vibrant Mughal gardens. From the iconic Dal Lake to the historic Shankaracharya Temple, the region's natural beauty and cultural richness create an ethereal tapestry, making Kashmir a paradise on Earth.

  • 7 Days 6 Nights, Group: 5 - 10 People, Kashmir

Kashmir, a poetic masterpiece, unfolds with the majesty of Gulmarg's snow-clad landscapes and the fragrant saffron fields of Pampore. The Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh gardens reveal Mughal opulence, while the floating markets of Dal Lake and the spiritual Hazratbal Shrine add unique dimensions to this Himalayan haven.

Tour Plan
  • Day 1: Arrive in Srinagar
    - Upon arrival, meet our representative and take a transfer to the hotel. Spend the day at leisure on boulevard road which is one of the most famous roads in Srinagar for tourists and is situated around the Dal Lake surrounded by Zabarwan mountain ranges. Also enjoy a 1-ho r shikara ride in Dal Lake at Nehru Park on boulevard road. Overnight stay in hotel.
  • Day 2: Srinagar - Sonmarg - Srinagar (84 kms / 2:30 hours approximately)
    - After breakfast leave with a full day excursion for Sonmarg “The meadow of gold. Situated at an altitude of 9000 Ft. Sonmarg is at a distance of 84 Kms from Srinagar is also called as “The meadow of gold”. Sonmarg lies in Sindh Valley streamed with flowers and surround d by mountains. The place is also the base for some interesting treks to the high altitude of Himalayan Lake. Ponies can be hired for the trip up to Thajiwas glacier, which is a major local attractio during the summer.Drive back to Srinagar. Overnight stay in hotel
  • Day 3: Srinagar- Gulmarg - Srinagar (140 Kms / 4 hours approximately)
    - After breakfast, drive to Gulmar “Meadow of Flowers” 2730 Mts. above sea level. The distance of 140 Kms will be covered in about 4 hrs. Gulmarg has one of the best Ski slopes in the world and the highest golf course of the world with 18 holes. One can also have the view of Nanga Parbhat if weather permits. The view enroute from Tangmarg to Gulmarg is fascinating. you can also have a short trek uptoKhilanmarg which Kongdori on a Cable Car Known as Gondola (on your own).Overnight at hotel
  • Day 4: Srinagar - pahalgam
    - After Breakfast Drive to Pahalgam “Valley of Shepherds”. On arrival check in at the Hotel in Pahalgam enjoy the nature and a mire the beauty of this quiet place an be in awe of the serene surroundings. One can also enjoy a pony ride to nearby places like Chandanwadi (on a direct hire basis) or if you are an avid nature lover you may take pleasure in a peaceful walk along the River Ladder. Pahalgam is famous for Pilgrimage. Overnight stay in hotel.
  • Day 5: Pahalgam (85 Kms / 2:30 hours approximately)
    - After Breakfast visit Betab Valley & Aru Valley, Mahabalashwer Temple, T lian Lake, Poshwan Park, Pahalgam Club Garden. Overnight stay in hotel
  • Day 6: Pahalgam Exploration- srinagar
    - After the breakfast, drive to Srinagar enjoys the site scene of the famous Mughal Gardens Chevnvshma Shahi “Royal Spring” Shalimar “Garden of Love” Nishat Garden “Garden of Pleasure” Shankaracharya Temple. On return visit a local Handicrafts Showroom for hand-knotted specialty silken carpets, shawls, jewelry ornaments. Overnight stay in srinagar Hotel.
  • Day 7: Airport drop
    After B/F depart to Airport to board your scheduled flight for onward destination.
