Place Of Golden Hearted People Golden Temple, Amritsar.

The Golden Temple in Amritsar, India, is a spiritual marvel. Adorned with gold leaf, its sacred sanctum, Harmandir Sahib, is surrounded by the shimmering Amrit Sarovar. The Guru Granth Sahib's recitation, the langar's communal meal, and the tranquil ambiance make it a spiritual oasis, embodying Sikh values of humility and equality.
