Tour Plan
  • Day 1: Airport Pickup
    Upon arrival at Dubai International Airport, we provide a “Meet & Assist” service. Transfer to hotel and check-in.
  • Day 2: Half-Day Dubai City Tour & Burj Khalifa 124th Floor Off-peak Hour With Dubai Mall Aquarium and Underwater Zoo ( Private Transfer)
    Breakfast at a hotel. In the morning, proceed on a half-day city tour of Dubai. The emirate of Dubai is located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf and is one of the seven emirates that make up the country. Experience the historic sites and vibrant cosmopolitan life of Dubai on this comprehensive sightseeing tour of the city. The new and old of Dubai which is the business, leisure, and sporting hub of the Middle East. It is the mystique of ancient Arabia shining alongside modern skyscrapers. Visit some of the city's most prominent landmarks like the famous Jumeirah Mosque. Jumeirah Mosque was built in 1979 and designed in the style of Fatimi mosques in Egypt. Tourists wishing to visit the mosques should wear appropriate clothing like trousers, long sleeves, and a scarf for ladies. Open to the public only on Sunday and Thursday. We stop at Jumeirah Public Beach, passing bay Jumeirah Beach Hotel, and stop for a photo at Burj Al Arab, the most luxurious and tallest standing hotel in the world. As we continue our journey and pass by the Sheikh's Mohamed palace, then take a short break by the creek to view the amazing architecture of Deira Towers. We then visit the fascinating underground Dubai Museum housed in the Al Fahidi Fort, built in 1800 from sea rocks and gypsum to defend the city. Inside, we feel the cooling effect of an old wind tower; wander past cabinets housing traditional khanjars(daggers) as well as musical instruments dated 300 BC. While in the basement you could view films of Dubai dated 1930’s, market life from the 1950s, desert life, and learn how the early pearl divers risked their lives for the bounty of the Gulf water. Experiencing ancient Arabia meticulously recreated in Bastakia-the oldest part of the city. Visit the aromatic Spice 'Souk'- a treasure trove of spices, and continue passing by the colorful textile market, and find yourself amazed at the sheer volume of dazzling gold jewelry at Gold Souq. There is no better way to get up close and personal with the world’s tallest structure than with the Burj Khalifa tour. Make your way to Downtown Dubai and gain access to Burj Khalifa via Dubai Mall. Then, climb to one of the world’s fastest elevators to enjoy an exciting ride up to the 124th floor. Get sweeping 360-degree views of Dubai’s mind-blowing landscape from its advanced Observation Deck, At the Top. Capture some amazing pictures and also learn interesting facts about Dubai’s history as well as this alluring structure on your visit here. Enter the world’s largest suspended aquarium. Admire thousands of aquatic animals from around the world, including rare species such as the King Crocodile and Sand Tiger Sharks. Discover three ecological zones: Rainforest, Rocky Shore, and Living Ocean.
  • Day 3: Desert Safari
    Enjoy our desert safari tour that provides you with an ultimate adventurous experience with Dune bashing on the sand dunes in a 4X4 vehicle, camel riding, various photographic opportunities, a belly dancing show, a Tanura dance show, and BBQ dinner with vegetarian and non-vegetarian food options. Special note: The pick-up/drop-off timing can be modified as per the trip schedule by 30 to 60 minutes, depending on your location and traffic conditions.
  • Day 4: Abu Dhabi City tour
    Get to know the UAE’s capital city and its fantastic historical, cultural, and thrilling sights with our Abu Dhabi city tour. With transfers arranged to and from Dubai, it allows you to discover Abu Dhabi’s diverse shades and persona. On an Abu Dhabi city tour, know how the capital city of the United Arab Emirates incredibly merges its rich ancient heritage with the most modern contemporary life. We’ve crafted this tour package with cautiously picked highlights of Abu Dhabi in order to ensure that you don’t miss out on taking in its significant sights. So be prepared to behold some of the most majestic landmarks of the UAE, as we pick you up from your hotel or preferred location in Dubai for a two-hour drive to Abu Dhabi. One sight that would for sure command your attention en route to Abu Dhabi city center is the astounding and expansive white structure - Sheik Zayed Grand Mosque which has been named to honor the late president, His Highness Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan. The tour will further lead you to attractions like the stupendous Emirates Palace, bustling the Abu Dhabi city center, and then to the mind-blowing Corniche boasting an impeccably manicured setting replete with kids’ play area, cafes, and restaurants. Before we head out to Abu Dhabi’s most modern attractions including Ferrari World and Yas Island, we’ll take you to Heritage Village which serves as a window to the region’s past. Eventually, the trip culminates with a visit to Abu Dhabi WTC Mall, where you’ll come across a whole lot of retail outlets selling unique, name-brand, and luxury items. Upon a full-day, unforgettable trip, we would drop you back to your rendezvous spot in Dubai.
  • Day 5: Dhow Cruise Marina dinner
    In the evening pick up from the hotel and proceed to enjoy the Dhow Dinner Cruise. Cruising down Dubai Creek is a novel experience, one which you cannot miss when in Dubai. Dividing the charming 'city of lights' into two commercial districts, Dubai Creek epitomizes the city's personality. Here, the magnificent sophistication of the 21st century walks hand in hand with the simplicity of a bygone era. Cruise on one of these traditional wooden vessels offers an intriguingly different view of this amazing city. The view of the city is intertwined with traditional and modern architecture and culture. You will see the splendid architecture of Dubai as the Sheikh's house. It is the birthplace of Sheikh Rashid and the Heritage village is reminiscent of days gone by. On the dhow cruise, an international buffet dinner will be served with soft drinks in silence Overnight stay at the hotel.
  • Day 6: Leisure Day Shopping
    A day especially dedicated for you to enjoy the stay and enjoy the facilities being provided or to shop around the markets of Dubai City.
  • Day 7: Departure
    At the time of your departure, Our Driver will Pick you up from your hotel in Dubai and drop you at the Dubai International Airport.
